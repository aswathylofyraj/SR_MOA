HPV corpus Exclusion Criteria:
- Children (age < 13 years) only.
- HPV-infected subjects only.
- Immunocompromised populations only (e.g., HIV-infected).
- Specific high-risk populations only (e.g., smokers, people who abuse alcohol).

PAPD corpus Exclusion Criteria:
- Patients with specific, non-targeted diseases or undergoing specific treatments only.
- Other special populations (e.g., prison inmates, immigrants, ethnic minority populations only).
- Language not English.
- Conference proceedings.
- No abstract.
- Narrative review (except highly relevant reviews for primary references).
- Small study (N < 25).
- Clinical guideline.
- Mixed pediatric/adult populations without segregated results.
